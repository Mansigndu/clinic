About Dr. Shailaja Sabnis

Dr. Shailaja Sabnis is a leading consultant rheumatologist and physician based in Mumbai, Maharashtra, with over 25 years of experience in diagnosing and treating a wide range of rheumatic diseases. She is committed to providing patient-centered care, focusing on accurate diagnosis, personalized treatment strategies, and ongoing support to improve the quality of life for her patients.

Dr. Sabnis, MD, is a rheumatology specialist in Mumbai. She treats conditions like Rheumatoid Arthritis, SLE, and provides pregnancy guidance for women with rheumatic diseases. Practicing at Sneh Nursing Home and affiliated with Lilavati & Nanavati Max, she delivers expert and compassionate care.
